### Playlist Submission
| Tool | Cost | Speed | Quality |
|------|------|-------|---------|
| **SubmitHub** | $1-3 per submission (premium) or free (standard) | Fast responses | High — curators are vetted |
| **Groover** | ~$2 per submission | 7-day guaranteed response | High — blogs + radio + curators |
| **Daily Playlists** | Free | Slow | Medium |

**My recommendation:** Start with **SubmitHub** ($20-30 budget for 10-15 premium submissions) + **Groover** ($10-20 for 5-10 submissions) + **Daily Playlists** and **Soundplate** (free). That's $30-50 total for solid initial push.

## BATCH WORKFLOW: WEEKLY CONTENT SYSTEM

### Sunday (Batch Day) — 2-3 Hours
1. **Film 3-5 videos** for the upcoming week
   - Use your phone, natural light, simple background
   - Film in vertical (9:16) for all platforms
   - Get multiple takes of each hook
2. **Edit all videos** using CapCut (free) or InShot (free)
   - Add captions/subtitles to every video (80% of people watch on mute)
   - Add your song as the audio track
3. **Schedule everything:**
   - Meta Business Suite → schedule IG + FB posts for Mon-Fri
   - TikTok Studio → schedule TikToks
   - YouTube Studio → schedule Shorts
4. **Write all captions** (or copy from CONTENT-BANK.md)
5. **Prepare Stories** for the week

### Monday-Friday (10-15 min/day)
- Check that scheduled post went live
- Reply to EVERY comment within 2 hours
- Watch Stories and engage with your audience
- Reshare any fan content to Stories

### Wednesday (Outreach Day) — 30 min
- Send 5 new playlist outreach DMs
- Follow up on 3 previous messages
- Check SubmitHub/Groover for responses

### Friday (Review Day) — 15 min
- Screenshot Spotify for Artists stats
- Note which content performed best
- Adjust next week's plan if needed
