Welcome — Let's Get You Set Up

To handle your distribution, registration, and outreach, Rollout Heaven needs access to your distribution and social accounts. Everything you submit is encrypted at rest and only viewable by Rollout Heaven through an audit-logged admin route.

How your credentials are stored: AES-256-GCM encryption with a server-only key. Never logged, never exposed to the frontend after submission, never sent to third parties. Only Rollout Heaven admins can decrypt them, and every access is audit-logged.
